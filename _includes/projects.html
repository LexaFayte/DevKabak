Depth Charge

Depth Charge is an deep sea eldritch bullet hell, and was developed in the first semester of the bachelor's degree program for PC using C++, OpenCL, Assembly, and Allegro. A team of four students contributed to the project:
Steven Chen, Alexandra Kabak, Christopher Klassen, Jack Tsai

Dive into the depths to defeat an ancient god that is flooding the Earth in Depth Charge, an eldritch bullet hell. Shoot your way through a sea of monsters, using elemental magic to exploit your enemies' weaknesses. As light fades and pressure mounts, can you survive this unforgiving crusade?

Roles for this project included level transitions, menus, file I/O, OpenCL implementation, player input and logic, enemy creation and logic.
